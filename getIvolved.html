Get Involved

At Assistive Hands Network Uganda, we believe that collective efforts drive meaningful change. By getting involved with our initiatives, you have the power to make a positive impact in the lives of people with disabilities. Explore the various ways you can contribute and become part of our mission:

Volunteer Opportunities

Talking Textbooks Project:

Join our "Talking Textbooks" initiative and become a digital voice for learners who are blind. Volunteer to narrate Ugandan textbooks and make education accessible to all. Our support team will guide you through the process. Your voice can open doors to learning.

Learn More

Fundraising Initiatives

Accessible Textbooks Initiative:

Help us bridge the gap of book poverty. Less than 1% of curriculum materials in Uganda are accessible to learners who are blind. Contribute to our initiative to create accessible books from mainstream textbooks. Your support ensures that every child has the chance to learn and thrive.

Learn More

Donation Options

Choose how you'd like to support our cause:

  • Monthly Donor: Choose to become a monthly donor and sustain our efforts in creating an inclusive society.
  • One-Time Donation: Make a one-time donation to support our programs and initiatives.
  • Program-Specific Donation: Direct your donation to a specific program that resonates with your passion and values.

Collaborations and Partnerships

We believe in the power of collaboration. If you share our vision of an inclusive and empowered society, collaborate with us on any of our diverse programs. Together, we can create lasting change that transforms lives. Send us an email at collaborations@assistivehandsnetwork.org to explore collaboration possibilities.
