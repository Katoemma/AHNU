What We Do

At Assistive Hands Network Uganda, we are dedicated to creating a more inclusive and empowered society for persons with disabilities. Our multifaceted programs address a range of challenges, each designed to uplift lives and nurture potential. Discover the essence of our initiatives and join us in weaving a tapestry of change:

Scholarships for Children: Breaking Barriers Through Education

Through our Scholarships for Children program, we offer a vital lifeline to children with disabilities, erasing the barriers that hinder their access to quality education. With comprehensive scholarships, we empower these bright minds to focus on learning, dreaming, and achieving their true potential.

Learn More

Empowering with Assistive Technology: Unlocking Possibilities

“Empowering with Assistive Technology” transforms lives through innovation. We equip children with disabilities with the tools they need to thrive academically and personally. By harnessing the power of technology, we shatter limitations, smash stereotypes, and champion equal access for all.

Learn More

Child Protection and Advocacy: Safeguarding Innocence

Our “Child Protection and Advocacy” initiative stands as a shield against abuse and exploitation. By engaging communities and training local leaders, we create safe havens for children with disabilities. Together, we forge a collective responsibility to protect their rights and dignity.

Learn More

SRH For All: Ensuring Reproductive Health for Everyone

“SRH For All” champions the reproductive health of persons with disabilities. Through accessible talks and community clinics, we break down the daunting barriers to essential services. Our mission is to empower every individual with the knowledge, the resources, and the rights to make informed choices about their sexual and reproductive health.

Learn More

Strong Hands: Crafting Empowerment through Livelihood Support

“Strong Hands” is our commitment to transforming lives through economic empowerment. By offering livelihood support and financial literacy, we guide individuals with disabilities towards self-sufficiency. Through mentorship and initial investment, we cultivate sustainable enterprises that thrive.

Learn More

These programs weave a tapestry of hope, empowerment, and change. Join us in our quest towards a more inclusive and equitable future.
