Scholarships for Children: Breaking the Chains of Injustice

The Problem: Children with Disabilities Face Multiple Barriers to Education in Uganda

Uganda is a land rich in culture and spirit, but it also bears the burden of being one of the world’s poorest nations, ranked 10th with a GNI per capita of a mere $700. Poverty affects millions of people in Uganda, but it hits especially hard for children with disabilities, who face multiple barriers to accessing quality education.

According to UNICEF, there are an estimated 2.5 million children with disabilities in Uganda, but only 9% of them attend primary school, and less than 1% attend secondary school. Many of them are excluded from formal education due to stigma, discrimination, lack of accessibility, inadequate resources most especially financial ones, and low awareness. As a result, they miss out on the opportunities and benefits that education can provide, such as improved health, income, and social inclusion.

But we firmly believe that poverty and disability should never dictate the destiny of a child.

The Solution: Providing Full Scholarships to Economically Disadvantaged Children with Disabilities

In the face of adversity, we stand as a beacon of hope, fighting the unforgiving grip of poverty that befalls children with disabilities. Our mission is simple yet profound: to extend a compassionate hand that lifts the weight of tuition fees, allowing these bright minds to focus on learning, dreaming, and achieving their true potential.

At Assistive Hands Network Uganda, we refuse to let poverty’s grip extinguish their aspirations. Instead, we offer a lifeline through comprehensive scholarships, ensuring that economic hardships don’t overshadow their dreams.

Our scholarships cover all the costs associated with education, such as school fees, uniforms, books, stationery, transport, and meals. We also provide additional support services, such as mentoring, counseling, health care, and disability aids. We work closely with our partner schools and communities to create an inclusive and supportive environment for our scholarship recipients.

The Impact: Empowering Children with Disabilities to Thrive Academically and Personally

Our scholarships open doors to education, empowering children with disabilities to thrive academically and personally. Among them, future leaders and changemakers emerge, excelling not only in the classroom but also in their communities. Each scholarship recipient is a testament to the resilience of the human spirit, proving that opportunity knows no bounds.

Since our inception in 202014, we have awarded over 100 scholarships to children with disabilities from various regions of Uganda. Our scholarship recipients have shown remarkable improvement in their academic performance and self-confidence. They have also participated in various extracurricular activities, such as sports, arts, clubs, and competitions. They have become role models and advocates for their peers and families.
