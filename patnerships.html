Partnerships: Collaborating for Change

At Assistive Hands Network Uganda, we believe that true impact is achieved through collaborative efforts. We are committed to forging partnerships and collaborations that amplify our mission of creating a more inclusive and empowered society for persons with disabilities. Together, we can make a difference that resonates far beyond our individual capacities.

Why Partner With Us?

By partnering with Assistive Hands Network Uganda, you become a catalyst for change. Our organization brings a wealth of expertise, experience, and dedication to the table. When you join hands with us, you tap into a network that is driven by a shared vision of breaking down barriers and uplifting lives.

Our partnerships and collaborations have resulted in:

  • Improved access to quality education for over 1,000 children with disabilities through scholarships, assistive technology, and inclusive environments.
  • Enhanced sexual and reproductive health for over 2,000 adolescents and adults with disabilities through accessible talks and community clinics.
  • Increased economic empowerment for over 500 individuals with disabilities through livelihood support and financial literacy.
  • Strengthened child protection for over 1,000 children with disabilities through community engagement and advocacy.

Ways to Partner

There are many ways you can partner with us to create a more inclusive and empowered society for persons with disabilities. Our partnership opportunities include:

  • Corporate Partnerships: As a corporate partner, your organization can contribute to our programs, initiatives, and events. Your support can drive tangible change while aligning with your corporate social responsibility goals.
  • NGO and Government Collaborations: Collaborate with us on joint initiatives that address the unique needs of persons with disabilities. Together, we can create comprehensive solutions that bring about lasting impact.
  • Educational Institutions: Partner with us to promote accessible education and inclusive environments. By working together, we can ensure that students with disabilities have equal access to quality education.
  • Community Organizations: Join forces with us to engage communities and create a collective commitment to support and protect persons with disabilities. Together, we can nurture safe and empowering spaces.

Our Partners

We express our endless gratitude to our esteemed partners who have already joined us on this journey of transformation. Their generous support and unwavering commitment have played a pivotal role in making our programs a reality.

Here are some of our partners and their contributions:

  • National Curriculum Development Center (NCDC) Uganda: Partnering to create 50 accessible and engaging books in audio and epub format for children with disabilities. We thank the Government of Uganda via NCDC for extending a hand of opportunity.
  • Kyambogo University Faculty of Special Needs: Partnering on a baseline study to gather reliable information and data on persons with disabilities in Uganda. We thank Kyambogo University for sharing their expertise and resources.
  • Robin Weld Foundation: Working together to ensure sexual and reproductive health for adolescents and adults with disabilities in Uganda. We thank Robin Weld Foundation for their vision and passion.

Do you want to learn more about our partners? Click on their names to visit their websites.

Are you interested in becoming a partner? Contact us at collaborations@assistivehandsnetwork.org.

Together, we can create a brighter future for persons with disabilities.
